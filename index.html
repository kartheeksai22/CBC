Our Story

Where Art Meets Memory

Chitram Bhalare Captures has been Rajahmundry's most trusted name in photography for years. Based in Aditya Nagar, we bring together artistic vision and technical mastery to create photographs that transcend time.

Our team specializes in South Indian ceremonies, traditions, and the vibrant cultural tapestry of Andhra Pradesh — with a deep respect for every moment's cultural significance and emotional depth.

With over 1,200 five-star reviews on Google, our work speaks for itself. We don't just take photos — we craft heirlooms.
